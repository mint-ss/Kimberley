Frequently Asked Questions

How to check policy and insurance terms?

1) After successful payment, please go to "My Account" → "More Orders" → "Vacation Insurance Orders" to download the electronic policy and insurance terms.

2) Specific coverage details and insurance limits can be found in the electronic policy and insurance terms. Electronic policies are legally valid.

3) Electronic policies have the same legal effect as paper policies and can be used for visa applications after color printing.

How to request an insurance invoice?

You can request an insurance invoice through your account dashboard. Go to "My Policies" and select the "Request Invoice" option for your specific policy. Invoices are typically available within 1-2 business days.
